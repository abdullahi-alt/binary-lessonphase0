strawberry cheesecake

ingredients

  • Graham crackers
  • sugar
  • butter
  • butter

preparation instruction

  1. Combine melted butter with sugar and graham cracker crumbs. The crumbs should resemble wet sand
  2. This recipe contains 4 8-ounce packages of cream cheese, but that’s not the only thing it needs
  3. A water bath creates steam inside the oven which helps cook the cheesecake without drying it out. It’s a method I swear by but it can be tricky
